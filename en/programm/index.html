Program

At the heart of our event is a large library with games for young and old, for strategists and enthusiasts. Discover new games with us, which we will explain to you on site without you having to take the rule book into your hands.

Free play is rounded off with program activities such as Swiss game developers, the popular Spielpass (recommended games for your to explore), flea market, role-playing games and tournaments.

The exact programme will be announced at a later date.

Do not hesitate to contact us via the contact form if you have an idea for the program.
